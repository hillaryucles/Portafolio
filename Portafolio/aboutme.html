About Me

I am a 21 year old college student from Honduras, with a strong passion for drawing and creating digitally. I am most skilled creating art on Adobe Illustrator and drawing by hand on Procreate, with my trusty iPad and Apple Pencil. My preferred art style could be considered cartoons. I loooove watching anime and animated movies and series are my favorite, my music taste is and was defined by my latino heritage, as reggaeton and also bachata, and a mix of many other genres I've discovered through my youth.
